For the three\nmonths ended May 31, 2004, software license fees, services and maintenance\nrevenues all showed increases. Cost of sales, software amortization and selling,\ngeneral and administrative expenses all increased for the quarter. This resulted\nin an operating income of $127,249, or $0.03 per fully diluted share, as\ncompared to $289,598, or $.06 per fully diluted share, in the year-ago quarter.\n\nFor the year ended May 31, 2004, net income decreased 13% to $421,814 or $.09\nper fully diluted share, as compared to $485,590 or $0.11 per fully diluted\nshare in the prior period. Software license revenues increased by approximately\n137% to $546,141 for the year ended May 31, 2004, as compared to $230,127 for\nthe year ended May 31, 2003. Revenue from services decreased by approximately 7%\nand hardware sales decreased by approximately 88%. Maintenance revenue increased\nby approximately 3%, as compared to the prior period. Software amortization\ndeclined by 1% and selling, general and administrative expenses decreased by 6%.\nThis resulted in operating income of $438,590, or $0.09 per fully diluted share,\nas compared to $517,271, or $0.11 per fully diluted share, in the year-ago\nperiod.\n\nDuring the period, the Company operated on a positive cash flow basis and,\nexcluding non-cash expenses, reported a net profit of $1,188,547 or $0.26 per\nfully diluted share, compared to a $1,259,596 or $0.28 per fully diluted share\nin the year-ago period.
