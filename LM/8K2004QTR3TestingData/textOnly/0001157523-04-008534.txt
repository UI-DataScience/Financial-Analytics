This marks Cohesant\'s\n14th consecutive quarter of record earnings.\n    Net income for the quarter was $541,724, an increase of 23.3% over\nthe $439,347 reported in the previous year period. On a fully diluted\nper share basis, net income was $.20 in the current quarter compared\nto $.17 last year. The Company\'s net sales for the third quarter of\nfiscal 2004 increased 12.0% to $5,116,265 from $4,570,128 for the same\nperiod last year.\n    For the first nine months of fiscal 2004, net income was\n$1,359,377, an increase of 36.1% over the $998,972 reported in the\nprevious year period. On a fully diluted per share basis, net income\nwas $.51 in the current period compared to $.39 last year. The\nCompany\'s net sales for the first nine months of fiscal 2004 increased\n16.9% to $14,641,856 from $12,526,006 for the same period last year.\n    Morris H. Wheeler, the Company\'s President and Chief Executive\nOfficer, stated, "Cohesant has again topped $5 million in quarterly\nsales and earned $0.20 cents on a fully diluted basis. Equipment sales\nat GlasCraft were up 27% over the same period last year in its\ndomestic market and GlasCraft\'s International equipment sales\ncontinued to be strong beating same period last year by 14%. Overall,\nEquipment sales were up over 17% for the quarter and are up over 14%\nfor the first nine months of this year compared to last. Coatings\nsales remained on a record pace for the year with over a 24% increase\nover the first nine months of fiscal 2004, but were essentially flat\nfor the quarter."\n    Cohesant Technologies Inc., based in Indianapolis, Indiana,\ndesigns, develops, and manufactures specialized dispense equipment and\nspecialty coating products through its two subsidiaries: GlasCraft,\nInc., and Raven Lining Systems, Inc. GlasCraft and, to a limited\nextent, Raven Lining Systems manufactures dispense equipment for\napplying plural component materials such as polyesters, polyurethanes,\npolyureas, and epoxies. Raven Lining Systems, Inc., manufactures and\nsells the Raven and AquataPoxy line of high-performance coating\nsystems. These systems provide corrosion protection and structural\nrenewal when coating and rehabilitating all types of infrastructure in\nwater, wastewater and industrial markets.\n\n\n\n
