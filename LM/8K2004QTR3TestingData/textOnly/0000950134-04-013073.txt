REVENUE INCREASES 48%  FOR SECOND QUARTER\n\nSaddle Brook, New Jersey, September 1, 2004 - Peoples Educational Holdings, Inc.\n("the Company") today reported revenue of $8.6 million, for its second quarter\nending June 30, 2004, up 48% from $5.8 million from the second quarter of 2003.\nSix months year-to-date revenue of $13.9 million increased 44%, as compared to\n$9.6 million in the comparable period of 2003. Net Income for the three and\nsix-month periods ended June 30, 2004 was $426,000, and $321,000, an increase of\n$312,000 and $329,000 respectively, from the same period in 2003. Earnings per\nshare, on a diluted basis, were $0.11 for the quarter as compared to $0.03 in\nthe second quarter of 2003. Six months year-to-date earnings per share, on a\ndiluted basis, were $0.08 as compared to $0.00 in the prior year.\n\n"We delivered continued strong growth for the second quarter of 2004 as well as\nfor our year-to-date period. Our growth continues to be driven by our\nstate-specific test preparation products, which grew to $5.2 million for the\nquarter and $9.2 million for the six months year-to-date, up 76% for the quarter\nand 63% for the six month period as compared to prior year," said Brian\nBeckwith, President and CEO. Instruction products for the student-at-risk and multicultural markets\n\nThe Company\'s proprietary products are supplemental in nature, meaning that they\nare predominately soft-cover, high gross profit margin titles that can be sold\nefficiently through the Company\'s direct sales force, as well as through\ncatalogs, direct mail, telemarketing, and independent commission sales\nrepresentatives.
