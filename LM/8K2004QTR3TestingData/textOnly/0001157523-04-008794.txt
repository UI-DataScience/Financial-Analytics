Eighteen month comparable restaurant sales increased\napproximately 8.7% compared to 2.8% in the third quarter a year ago. Twelve\nmonth comparable sales increased 7.5% and 2.0% for the third quarter of 2004 and\n2003, respectively.\n    On its July 22, 2004 conference call, the Company forecasted an eighteen\nmonth third quarter same store sales increase of approximately 4.5% to 5.5% with\na corresponding earnings per fully diluted share in the range of $0.22- $0.24.\nIn line with previously announced comments, however, several one-time charges\nwill be incurred during the quarter relating to three store closures, costs\nassociated with anticipated legal resolutions and accelerated depreciation for\nstore remodel candidates. The exact scope of these one-time charges will be\ndetermined in the coming weeks and will be discussed at length on California\nPizza Kitchen Inc.\'s upcoming third quarter conference call. Excluding these\ncharges, however, management is now comfortable increasing its earnings guidance\nfrom continuing operations to $0.25 - $0.26 per fully diluted share.\n    The Company intends to release its third quarter earnings on October 21,\n2004 at approximately 4:00 p.m. EDT with a conference call to follow at\napproximately 5:00 p.m. EDT. Management will specifically address full year and\nfourth quarter guidance at that time. A webcast of the conference call can be\naccessed at www.cpk.com.\n    California Pizza Kitchen, Inc., founded in 1985, is a leading casual dining\nchain in the premium pizza segment. The Company\'s full service restaurants\nfeature an imaginative line of hearth-baked pizzas, including the original BBQ\nChicken Pizza, and a broad selection of distinctive pastas, salads, soups and\nsandwiches. The average guest check is approximately $11.84. The chain operates,\nlicenses or franchises 169 restaurants as of September 27, 2004, of which 140\nare company-owned and 29 operate under franchise or license agreements. The\nCompany also has a licensing arrangement with Kraft Pizza Company which\nmanufactures and distributes a line of California Pizza Kitchen premium frozen\npizzas.\n    California Pizza Kitchen, Inc. can be found on the internet at\nwww.cpk.com.\n\n    This press release includes certain "forward-looking statements" within the\nmeaning of the Private Securities Litigation Reform Act of 1995.
