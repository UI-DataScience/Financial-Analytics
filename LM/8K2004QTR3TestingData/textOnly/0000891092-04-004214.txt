Moves From Semi-Annual to Quarterly Cash Dividends;\n                            Doubles Annual Payout\n\n    ATLANTA, Aug. 23 /PRNewswire-FirstCall/ -- Aaron Rents, Inc. (NYSE: RNT),\nthe nation\'s leader in the sales and lease ownership, specialty retailing and\nrental of residential and office furniture, consumer electronics, home\nappliances and accessories, today announced that it is increasing the\nfrequency of its historical semi-annual cash dividends to quarterly cash\ndividends. The next\ndividend payable will be $.013 per share on Common Stock and $.013 per share\non Class A Common Stock, payable October 1, 2004, to shareholders of record as\nof the close of business on September 1, 2004.  It is the intent of the\nCompany to now begin paying quarterly dividends of $.013 per share on both\nCommon Stock and Class A Common Stock.\n    "Based upon the current outstanding performance of the Company and our\nfuture outlook, the Board of Directors decided to substantially increase the\ndividend to shareholders and begin a quarterly dividend policy," said R.\nCharles Loudermilk, Sr., Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Aaron Rents.\n"These actions are an indication of the confidence we have in the future\nprospects of the Company."\n    Aaron Rents, Inc., based in Atlanta, currently has over 950 Company-\noperated and franchised stores in the United States, Puerto Rico, and Canada\nfor the rental and sale of residential and office furniture, accessories,\nconsumer electronics and household appliances.
