ANNOUNCES RECORD THIRD QUARTER AND NINE MONTH RESULTS\n\n                      REVENUE UP 147% QUARTER-OVER-QUARTER\n\n\nLAGUNA HILLS, Calif., Sept. 13, 2004 (PRIMEZONE via COMTEX) -- Superclick, Inc.\n(OTCBB:SPCK) today announced record results for the quarter and nine months\nended July 30, 2004.\n\nNet revenue for the quarter was $804,701, up from $324,768, or up 147% on a\nquarter-over quarter basis. Net loss was $54,440, down from $430,800, or 87%\nover last quarter. These results are in line with the company\'s previous raised\nguidance that was announced on August 16, 2004. Net revenue for the nine months\nended was $1,420,891 and net loss for the period was $597,890, or $0.03 per\nshare.\n\nGross margins for the quarter were 43%, up from 25% over the second quarter of\n2004. Sequentially, margins improved by 72%. Gross margins for the nine months\nended July 30 were 39%.\n\nChief Executive Officer and President of Superclick, John Glazik, remarked that\n"We\'ve had a terrific quarter marked by near-perfect execution of our sales and\ndeployment teams. Our new reseller partnerships are beginning to bring in\nsubstantial business and we have achieved a much higher level of awareness in\nthe marketplace as the "best in class" solution. Overall, this marks a\nbreakthrough quarter for the company."\n\nQUARTERLY BUSINESS HIGHLIGHTS\n\n      o     Major installations continuing in Canada.\n\n      o     Business through Verizon reseller arrangement beginning to ramp -\n            The company began to execute its first material purchase orders from\n            Verizon.\n\n      o     Gross margins improved due to a favorable product mix.\n\n      o     The company expanded its distribution channels by adding direct\n            sales, resellers and agents worldwide.\n\nAs previously announced, the company expects revenues to increase sequentially\nto within a range of $2.25 to $2.5 million for the fiscal year.\n\nAbout Superclick, Inc.\n\nSuperclick, Inc. (OTCBB:SPCK), through its wholly owned, Montreal-based\nsubsidiary Superclick Networks, Inc., develops, manufactures, markets and\nsupports the Superclick Internet Management System (SIMS(tm)) in worldwide\nhospitality, multi-tenant unit (MTU) and university markets. Superclick provides\nhotels, MTU residences and universities with cost-effective Internet access\nutilizing high-speed DSL, CAT5 wiring, wireless and dial-up modem technologies.\nSuperclick\'s proprietary technology converts dial-up analog Internet calls to\ndigital access, improves connection speeds, unclogs local trunks, consolidates\nInternet traffic, supports flexible billing and provides targeted advertising to\nend-users. Current clients include MTU residences and Crowne Plaza(r), Four\nPoints by Sheraton(r), InterContinental Hotels Group PLC, Hilton(r), Holiday\nInn(r), Holiday Inn Express(r), Hampton Inn(r), Marriott(r), Novotel(r),\nRadisson(r), Sheraton(r), Westin(r) and Wyndham(r) hotels in Canada and the\nUnited States.\n\nSafe Harbor Statement\n\nStatements in this press release that are not statements of historical or\ncurrent fact constitute "forward-looking statements" within the meaning of the\nPrivate Securities Litigation Reform Act of 1995.
