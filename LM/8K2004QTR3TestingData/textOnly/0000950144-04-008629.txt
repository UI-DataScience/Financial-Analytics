ANNOUNCES RECORD SECOND QUARTER AND\n                         SIX MONTH REVENUES AND EARNINGS\n\n      -     SECOND QUARTER NET INCOME UP 45.1% TO A RECORD $35 MILLION\n\n      -     REVENUES ROSE 49.4% TO A RECORD $512 MILLION FOR THE SIX MONTHS\n\n      -     NET INCOME CLIMBED 48.8% TO A RECORD $71 MILLION FOR THE SIX MONTHS\n\n      -     AUGUST COMPARABLE STORE SALES CURRENTLY IN THE 6%- 8% RANGE\n\n      FORT MYERS, FL - AUGUST 26, 2004 - Chico\'s FAS, Inc. (NYSE: CHS) today\nannounced its financial results for the second quarter and six months ended July\n31, 2004.\n\n      Net sales for the second quarter ended July 31, 2004, increased 46.9% to a\nrecord $255 million from $173 million for the second quarter ended August 2,\n2003. Net income rose 45.1% to $35 million, or $0.39 a diluted share, compared\nto net income of $24 million, or $0.28 a diluted share, in the prior year\'s\nsecond quarter. Comparable store sales for the Company-owned stores increased\n14.1% for the thirteen-week period ended July 31, 2004, compared to the same\nthirteen-week period last year.\n\n      For the six months ended July 31, 2004, net sales increased 49.4% to a\nrecord $512 million from $342 million for the prior year\'s six months ended\nAugust 2, 2003. Net income rose 48.8% to $71 million, or $0.79 a diluted share,\ncompared to net income of $48 million, or $0.55 a diluted share, in the prior\nperiod. Comparable store sales for Company-owned stores increased 17.0% for the\ntwenty-six week period compared to the same twenty-six week period last year.\n\n      Scott A. Edmonds, President and CEO, commented, "Our second quarter\nfinancial results demonstrate the continued strength and momentum of the Chico\'s\nbrand. We are extremely pleased with the Company\'s record net sales, net income,\nand comparable store sales results. Our industry leading operating margin of\n22.3% for the second consecutive quarter further demonstrates our ability to\nconsistently deliver strong results to our shareholders. In addition, we are\nvery pleased with our current August same store sales results and the strong\nreaction we are seeing to the introduction of our Fall merchandise."\n\n      "The White House|Black Market brand again contributed between $.02 and\n$.025 to our $.39 diluted earnings per share and we are looking forward to the\nlaunch of a frequent\n\n\n                                   Page 1 of 4\n', u'\nshopper club for their vast customer base. We feel stronger than ever about the\nWhite House|Black Market brand\'s potential to bring significant long-term value\nto our shareholders."\n\n      Mr. Edmonds further stated, "Our financial strength and ability to\nleverage expertise in numerous disciplines of the business provides us with the\nopportunity to continue to grow and extend the Chico\'s brand. As we begin the\nlaunch of our new intimate apparel concept, "Soma by Chico\'s," we look forward\nto providing our customer with the outstanding product, personalized service,\nand unique shopping experience she has come to know and enjoy at Chico\'s. Even\nthough our accomplishments to date have been significant, we believe that our\nbest and biggest opportunities still lie ahead."\n\n      Chico\'s sells exclusively designed, private-label women\'s clothing and\nrelated accessories. The Company operates 603 women\'s specialty stores,\nincluding stores in 47 states, the District of Columbia, the Virgin Islands and\nPuerto Rico operating under the Chico\'s, White House | Black Market and Soma by\nChico\'s names. The Company owns 425 Chico\'s front-line stores, 24 Chico\'s outlet\nstores, 139 White House | Black Market front-line stores, 1 White House | Black\nMarket outlet store and 3 Soma by Chico\'s stores; franchisees own and operate 11\nChico\'s stores.\n\n      Certain statements contained herein, including without limitation,\n      statements addressing the beliefs, plans, objectives, estimates or\n      expectations of the Company or future results or events constitute\n      "forward-looking statements" within the meaning of the Private Securities\n      Litigation Reform Act of 1995, as amended.
