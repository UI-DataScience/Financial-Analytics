A. Bank Clothiers\' Earnings Increase 75%\n\n    HAMPSTEAD, Md.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Sept. 9, 2004--JoS. A. Bank\nClothiers, Inc. (Nasdaq National Market: "JOSB") announced net income\nof $3.5 million for the second quarter ended July 31, 2004, compared\nwith net income of $2.0 million in the second quarter of FY2003 -\nrepresenting an increase of 75% and another record quarter for the\nCompany when compared with the prior-year period. Earnings per share\nincreased to $0.25 in the most recent quarter, compared with $0.14 in\nthe three months ended August 2, 2003. For the first half of FY2004,\nnet income increased 112% to $8.9 million, versus $4.2 million, while\nearnings per share reached $0.62, compared with $0.30 in the\ncorresponding period of the previous fiscal year.\n    "Strong second quarter operating results were driven by\ndouble-digit comp-store sales gains, continued strength in catalog and\nInternet sales, new product innovations, the growing popularity of our\nluxurious Signature and Signature Gold product lines, and a dynamic\nnew store opening program," stated Robert N. Wildrick, Chief Executive\nOfficer of JoS. A. Bank Clothiers, Inc.\n    "We opened 20 new stores during the six months ended July 31,\n2004," continued Wildrick. "Our plans call for the opening of\napproximately 40 additional stores during the second half of the\nfiscal year, including at least four more stores each in California,\nTexas and Florida and our first stores in Seattle, Washington. Also,\nthe Company\'s new brand-building media campaign, which will play a key\nrole in positioning JoS. A. Bank as the premiere upscale menswear\nbrand in the U.S., will be launched in November 2004 with a series of\ntelevision and selective magazine advertisements."\n    As previously reported, total sales for the second quarter ended\nJuly 31, 2004 increased 27.3% to $82.0 million, compared with $64.4\nmillion in the second quarter of 2003. Comparable store sales\nincreased 10.2% in the second quarter of 2004, while combined catalog\nand Internet sales rose 17.9%. Total sales for the six months ended\nJuly 31, 2004 increased 27.8% to $161.9 million, compared with $126.7\nmillion in the first half of the previous fiscal year. Comparable\nstore sales increased 11.9% in the six months ended July 31, 2004,\nwhile combined catalog and Internet sales rose 18.3%.\n    All earnings per share amounts discussed herein refer to diluted\nearnings per share and reflect the impact of a 50% stock dividend that\nwas distributed to stockholders on February 18, 2004 and a 25% stock\ndividend that was distributed to stockholders on August 18, 2004.\n    A conference call to discuss the Company\'s operating results is\nscheduled for 11:00 A.M. Eastern Time, today, September 9, 2004. To\nparticipate in the conference call today, please call 888-428-4479 a\nfew minutes before 11:00 a.m. Eastern Time. A replay of the conference\ncall will be available until September 16, 2004 by dialing (USA)\n800-475-6701 or (International) 320-365-3844. The access code for the\nreplay will be 740555.\n\n    JoS. A. Bank Clothiers, Inc., established in 1905, is one of the\nnation\'s leading retailers of men\'s classically-styled tailored and\ncasual clothing, sportswear, footwear and accessories. The Company\nsells its full product line through 237 stores in 36 states and the\nDistrict of Columbia, a nationwide catalog, and an e-commerce website\nthat can be accessed at www.josbank.com. The Company is headquartered\nin Hampstead, MD, and its common stock is listed on the Nasdaq\nNational Market under the symbol "JOSB".\n\n    The Company\'s statements concerning future operations contained\nherein are forward-looking statements within the meaning of the\nPrivate Securities Litigation Reform Act of 1995.
