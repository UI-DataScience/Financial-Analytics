The decline\nwas partially due to price increases from the Company\x92s ceiling fan\nmanufacturer that were not passed through to customers. The gross margin of\nthe mass retail division decreased to 22.4% of sales for the three months ended\nJune\xa030, 2004, compared to 26.1% of sales in the fourth quarter of 2003.
