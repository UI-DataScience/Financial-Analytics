month of June\xa02004. The\ndistribution is payable September\xa015, 2004, to unit holders of record as of\nAugust\xa031, 2004. The average gas price may vary\nsignificantly from the posted index price for the San Juan Basin. The index\nprice is a gross sales price, and the revenues used in the calculation of\naverage gas prices are net of transportation, processing and gathering costs.\nFurthermore, the distribution to the Trust in any given month may include\nsignificant volume adjustments for sales in prior months that reflect pricing\nfor those prior months.
