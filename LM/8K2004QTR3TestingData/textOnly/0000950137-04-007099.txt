ANNOUNCES ROYALTY AGREEMENT WITH RYER, INC.\nAND SIX MONTH PERIOD ENDED JUNE 30, 2004 FINANCIAL RESULTS\n\nSan Diego, CA-August 24, 2004-On August 16, 2004, Planet Polymer Technologies,\nInc. (OTC BB: POLY.OB) a San Diego advanced materials company ("Planet")\nreported a loss for the six months ending June 30, 2004 of $244,591 and a loss\nfrom operations of $255,306 compared to net income of $181,202 and a loss from\noperations of $106,286 for the same period in 2003. Planet incurred increased\naccounting and legal expenses due to the proposed transaction between Planet and\nAllergy Free, LLC. For the six months ended June 30, 2004 loss per share was\n$0.04, compared to net income of $0.03 per share for the same period in 2003.\n\nIn addition, Planet reported that the patent rights relating to the AQUAMIM(R)\nproducts were assigned, with Planet\'s approval, by Ryer Enterprises, LLC to\nRyer, Inc. As part of the assignment, Planet received a payment from Ryer, Inc.\nof approximately $161,000 to satisfy the remaining balance of the note relating\nto the AQUAMIM(R) products and the original Royalty Agreement between Planet and\nRyer Enterprises, LLC.\n\nOn August 13, 2004, Planet filed an Amended 10KSB for the annual period ending\nDecember 31, 2003, and an Amended 10QSB for the three month period ending March\n31, 2004.\n\n      For further information contact H.
