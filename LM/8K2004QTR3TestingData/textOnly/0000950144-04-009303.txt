Church's average check was up 7.3 percent and 9.1 percent,\nrespectively, Popeyes average check was also up 5.9 percent and 5.7 percent,\nrespectively, and Cinnabon was up 5.7 percent and 5.0 percent, respectively.\n\n\nAFC experienced a year over year decline in the number of transactions in all of\nthe brands.
