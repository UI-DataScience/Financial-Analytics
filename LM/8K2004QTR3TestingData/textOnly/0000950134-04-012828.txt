REPORTS SECOND QUARTER FINANCIAL RESULTS\n\n         Chicago, Illinois, August 26, 2004 -- Whitehall Jewellers, Inc.\n(NYSE:JWL) today reported financial results for the second quarter ended July\n31, 2004.\n\n         The Company reported total second quarter sales of $72.3 million\ncompared to $72.7 million for the second quarter last year. Net loss for the\nsecond fiscal quarter of 2004 was $3.2 million compared to net loss of $2.8\nmillion for the same period a year ago. Net loss per share of $0.23 in the\nsecond quarter compares to net loss per share of $0.20 for the second quarter of\nlast year.\n\n         As previously disclosed, the Company had recorded in the fourth quarter\nof last year and the first quarter of the current fiscal year a total litigation\naccrual of $8.9 million for the consolidated Capital Factors actions and related\ninvestigations by the United States Attorney and Securities and Exchange\nCommission. Since that time, the Company has engaged in additional settlement\ndiscussions relating to these matters. In light of developments regarding the\nforegoing, the Company has recorded an additional accrual in the quarter ended\nJuly 31, 2004 in the amount of $310,000. In addition, professional fees\nincreased by approximately $370,000 over the second quarter last year. This\nincrease was incurred primarily in connection with the consolidated Capital\nFactors actions and the related investigations by the United States Attorney and\nSecurities and Exchange Commission.\n\n         For the six-month period ended July 31, 2004, the Company reported\nsales of $145.3 million compared to $141.9 million last year. Net loss was $6.9\nmillion versus a net loss of $5.6 million for the same period a year ago.
