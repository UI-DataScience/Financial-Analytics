TO REPURCHASE\n                   UP TO AN ADDITIONAL 5% OF ITS COMMON SHARES\n                   -------------------------------------------\n\nCLARKSTON, WA - August 27, 2004 - FirstBank NW Corp. (Nasdaq: FBNW), the holding\ncompany for FirstBank Northwest, today announced that its Board of Directors has\nauthorized the repurchase of up to 5% of its common stock, or approximately\n147,866. The new program will begin immediately, and shares will be repurchased\nfrom time to time in the open market depending on market conditions and the\ncapital requirements of FirstBank NW Corp., and in accordance with securities\nlaws and regulations The stock repurchase program announced on November 21, 2003\nwas completed in August 2004.\n\n"The repurchase is consistent with the Board\'s ongoing capital management plan,"\nsaid Clyde E. Conklin, President and Chief Executive Officer. "We evaluate all\noptions on a regular basis in order to ensure we are consistent with our\noperating plan and our shareholders\' interests. Stock repurchases are recognized\nas an excellent method to enhance trading liquidity and stock value. We\nanticipate repurchases within this authorization to be managed within compliance\nof our capital policy, and as our operating plan is executed." This repurchase\nprogram is the second since the completion of the acquisition of Oregon Trail\nFinancial Corp. by FirstBank NW Corp. on October 31, 2003. Since July 1998,\nFirstBank NW Corp. has completed repurchases totaling 896,242 shares.\n\nAs reported previously, the Board of Directors declared a cash dividend of $0.17\nper share for shareholders of record on August 11, 2004, which was paid on\nAugust 25, 2004.\n\nFirstBank NW Corp. is the parent of FirstBank Northwest. Founded in 1920,\nFirstBank Northwest is based in Clarkston, Washington and operates 20 branch\nlocations in Idaho, Washington, and Oregon. FirstBank Northwest is known as the\nlocal community bank, offering its customers highly personalized service in the\nmany communities it serves.
