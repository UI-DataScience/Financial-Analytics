Thermal\nmanagement products, principally heat sinks, dissipate unwanted heat generated\nby electronic components. The Company\'s thermal management products serve the\nautomotive, telecommunication, industrial controls, transportation, power\nsupply, factory automation, consumer electronics, aerospace, defense,\nmicroprocessor, and computer industries.
