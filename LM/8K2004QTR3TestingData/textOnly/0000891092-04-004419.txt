Announces Results for Three and Nine Months Ended\n                                 July 31, 2004\n\n    WILLISTON, Vt., Sept. 13 /PRNewswire-FirstCall/ -- Vermont Pure Holdings,\nLtd. announced today that it will file its results for the three and nine\nmonth fiscal periods ending July 31, 2004 on Form 10-Q with the Security and\nExchange Commission and will hold its quarterly conference call tomorrow.\n\n    Sales for the third quarter ended July 31, 2004 increased 2% to $13.6\nmillion from $13.4 during the same period in 2003.  Sales for the nine months\nended July 31, 2004 increased 5% to $38.7 million from $36.8 million for the\nsame period in 2003.  The increase in sales was attributable to the\nintegration of recent acquisitions into the base business.\n\n    Income from continuing operations was $390,000 for the fiscal third\nquarter of 2004 versus $420,000 in the third quarter of fiscal year 2003.\nIncome from continuing operations was $256,000 for the first nine months of\nthe current fiscal year compared to $859,000 in the first nine months of\nfiscal year 2003.  The decrease in both periods was attributable to lower\ngross profit due to lower selling prices, a shift toward increased sales of\nlower margin products, and higher transportation, fuel, and insurance costs\nfor the period.\n\n    Net income, including income from discontinued operations, for the three\nmonths ended July 31, 2004 was $390,000, or $.02 per share, compared to net\nincome of $566,000, or $.03 per share for the corresponding period in 2003.\nFor the nine months ended July 31, 2004, net income was $416,000, or $.02 per\nshare, compared to $1.2 million, or $.05 per share for the corresponding\nperiod in 2003.  Net income for the nine months of 2004 included a gain on the\nsale of the retail segments of business, before taxes, of $353,000.\n\n    Tomorrow's conference call will start at 10:00 A.M.
