<P ALIGN=CENTER><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE="2"><B>Cognos<SUP>&reg; </SUP>Delivers Record Second Quarter Revenue and Earnings</B> </FONT> </P>

<P ALIGN=CENTER><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE="2"><B>21 Percent Growth in License Revenue Drives 52 Percent Increase in Net Income</B> </FONT> </P>

<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2><B>Ottawa, ON &amp; Burlington, MA,
September 22, 2004 &#150; </B>Cognos Incorporated (Nasdaq: COGN;&nbsp;TSX: CSN &#150; all
figures in U.S. dollars), the world leader in business intelligence (BI) and corporate
performance management (CPM) solutions, today announced record results for its second
quarter of fiscal year 2005, ended August 31, 2004. </FONT></P>



<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Revenue for the quarter was $185.2
million, an increase of 17 percent from the second quarter of last fiscal year, when
revenue was $158.2 million. Net income for the quarter was $27.6 million, resulting in
diluted earnings per share of $0.30. This is an increase of 52 percent compared with net
income of $18.2 million and diluted earnings per share of $0.20 in the second quarter of
last fiscal year. </FONT></P>



<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>License revenue achieved strong
growth in the quarter, reaching $75.4 million. This was an increase of 21 percent compared
with license revenue of $62.2 million in the second quarter of last fiscal year. </FONT></P>



<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>Revenue for the first six months of
fiscal year 2005, ended August 31, 2004, was $358.8 million, an increase of 16 percent
compared with revenue of $308.7 million for the first six months of last fiscal year. Net
income for the first six months of this fiscal year was $47.7 million or $0.51 per share.
This compares to net income of $30.5 million or $0.33 per share in the first six months of
last fiscal year. </FONT></P>
