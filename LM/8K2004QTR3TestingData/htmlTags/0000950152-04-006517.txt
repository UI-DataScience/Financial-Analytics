<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Sparton Corporation Releases Fiscal 2004 Fourth Quarter Results


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">JACKSON, Mich.&#151;(BUSINESS WIRE)&#151;Aug. 27, 2004&#151;Sparton Corporation (NYSE:SPA)
announced financial results for the fourth quarter and fiscal year ended June
30, 2004.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">BDO Seidman, auditors for Sparton Corporation, have substantially completed
their audit for the fiscal year ended June&nbsp;30, 2004, and we are furnishing the
following results.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Sales for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2004 were $47.8&nbsp;million versus $49.0
million last year. The decrease in sales primarily reflected a lowered demand
for homeland security products, with some offset in the aerospace markets. For
fiscal year 2004, the Company reported net sales of $161.0&nbsp;million, compared to
$169.9 last year. For the year, governmental sales declined 11% to $44.8
million, industrial/other sales decreased 29% to $39.9&nbsp;million, and
medical/scientific instrumentation sales decreased 4% to $17.9&nbsp;million. The
decrease in government sales was generally attributable to a decline in foreign
demand, including one delayed foreign sonobuoy sale of $4.7&nbsp;million, which, due
to unforeseen shipping complications did not occur until July. The decrease in
industrial/other sales was due to reduced demand for chemical trace detection
equipment in both the U.S. and Canadian airports. These sales approximated
$2.9&nbsp;million in fiscal 2004 compared to $25.5&nbsp;million last year. Aerospace
sales increased during fiscal 2004 by 30% to $58.4&nbsp;million, primarily due to
increased demand from one existing customer.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Net income for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2004 was $1.5&nbsp;million ($.18 per
share, basic and diluted) compared to $2.6&nbsp;million ($.31 per share basic, $.30
diluted) last year. For the full fiscal year, the Company&#146;s net loss was $2.0
million ($.24 per share, basic and diluted) compared to a profit of $9.0
million ($1.08 per share basic, $1.07 diluted) last year. Fiscal 2003&#146;s net
income includes the $5.5&nbsp;million favorable settlement, ($3.6&nbsp;million net of tax), with
the U.S. Department of Energy and others that was recorded in September&nbsp;2002.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">At June&nbsp;30, 2004, total shareowners&#146; equity was $88.9&nbsp;million. The Company had
no outstanding long or short-term debt, and cash and cash equivalents and
investment securities totaled $29.5&nbsp;million.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The Board of Directors has scheduled Wednesday, November&nbsp;10, 2004, as the date
for the Sparton Annual Shareowners&#146; Meeting. The annual meeting will be held in
Jackson, Michigan. Sparton expects to mail the annual report to shareowners in
early October.
