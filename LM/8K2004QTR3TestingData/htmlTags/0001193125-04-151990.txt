<TD ALIGN="left" VALIGN="top"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><I>Third consecutive quarter of operating profits; net profit of $427,000</I> </FONT></TD></TR></TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>ALPHARETTA, GEORGIA &#150; Sept. 2, 2004</B> &#150; Optio Software (OTCBB:OPTO.OB), a leading provider of document output management and forms automation solutions,
today announced financial results for the quarter ended July 31, 2004. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman"
SIZE="2"><B><I>Quarterly results </I></B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Net income was $427,000, compared to
net income of $646,000 reported during the same quarter of the previous fiscal year. Income before income taxes was $427,000 and $494,000 in the three months ended July 31, 2004 and 2003, respectively. Diluted earnings per share and earnings per
basic share were $0.02. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B><I>Software, services and maintenance revenues and
costs of revenue </I></B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Overall revenues rose 1 percent year-over-year.
Software license revenues increased 3 percent to $2.6 million, compared with $2.5 million in the second quarter of the prior year. Services and maintenance revenues remained consistent at $4.2 million in both the three months ended July 31, 2004 and
July 31, 2003. Total cost of software, services and maintenance revenues were $1.5 million, a 2 percent decline from the $1.6 million reported during the same quarter in the prior year. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B><I>Operating expenses </I></B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Total operating expenses increased from $4.7 million in the three months ended July 31, 2003 to $4.9 million in the three months ended July 31, 2004. Optio has continued
its strategy of reinvesting improved cash flows back into sales and marketing and research and development activities, while continuing to reduce general and administrative expenses. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B><I>Year-to-date results </I></B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Net income for the six months ended July 31, 2004 was $995,000, a 19 percent increase over the $839,000 net income in the six months ended July 31, 2003. Basic earnings
per share and diluted earnings per share for the six months ended July 31, 2004 were $0.05 and $0.04, respectively. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P>

<p Style='page-break-before:always'>
<HR  SIZE="3" COLOR="#999999" WIDTH="100%" ALIGN="CENTER">
 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B><I>Software, services and maintenance revenues and costs of revenue </I></B></FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Software license revenues increased 19 percent from $4.6 million in the six months ended
July 31, 2003 compared to $5.5 million in the six months ended July 31, 2004. Services, maintenance and other revenue declined 1 percent to $8.5 million for the six months ended July 31, 2004, compared to $8.6 million for the six-month period ended
July 31, 2003. Total revenue increased 6 percent to $14.0 million, compared to $13.2 million for the same period in the prior year. Total costs of revenue remained constant at $3.3 million in both the six months ended July 31, 2004 and 2003.
</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B><I>Operating expenses </I></B></FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Total operating expenses increased to $9.8 million for the six months ended July 31, 2004,
