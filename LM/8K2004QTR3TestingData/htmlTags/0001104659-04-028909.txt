<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">quarter.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Revenues for

its working capital position by $11.7 million during the fiscal year.&#160; In addition, the company recently renewed a
$7 million working capital credit facility from Textron Financial Corporation
with more flexible availability and better rate terms.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Including a
goodwill write-off of $11.6 million, the company&#146;s net loss was $19 million in
fiscal 2004, as compared to $13.8 million in fiscal 2003.&#160; The net loss per share for the 2004 fiscal
year was $0.40, as compared to the prior year loss per share of $0.38.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-autospace:none;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;Over the past
year, we have successfully reduced operating costs, and improved our capital
position,&#148; said Steve Ross, DynTek&#146;s chief executive officer.&#160; &#147;Aside from the large write-down of goodwill
and one-time non-recurring items, the company&#146;s results during the last fiscal
quarter showed marked improvement over the prior quarter.&#160; Through government contracts, we have
amassed a revenue base in several key regions.&#160;
In addition, we are aggressively pursuing a growth strategy to enable us
to expand our capabilities in the commercial market and broaden our geographic
footprint.&#160; Our merger and acquisition
strategy, combined with organic growth, should also contribute to our top line
revenue numbers, profitability, and sales and technical capabilities, and
provide us with additional critical mass needed to enhance shareholder value.&#148;</font></p>
