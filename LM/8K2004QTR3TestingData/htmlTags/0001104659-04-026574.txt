<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">IOMED
Reports Fourth Quarter and Fiscal 2004 Operating Results</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<h3 style="font-weight:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;text-align:center;"><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;">Record Sales and Net Income</font></i></h3>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-family:Times New Roman;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Salt Lake City, UT (August 30,
2004) &#151; IOMED, Inc.</font></b><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;"> (AMEX:IOX) today reported financial results for the
fourth quarter and fiscal year ended June 30, 2004. The Company posted record
quarterly and annual sales and net income, as well as positive operating cash
flow.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<h4 style="font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Fourth Quarter Results</font></b></h4>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">For the quarter ended June 30, 2004, sales
were $3,163,000, marking the third consecutive quarter of sales in excess of $3
million. Revenues from increased unit volume were offset, in part, by dealer
price incentives introduced during the quarter. Gross margin was 62% compared
with 64% a year ago.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Total operating expenses of $1,603,000 were
down 8% from the prior-year quarter. Selling, general and administrative
expenses decreased 12% during the quarter, while the Company&#146;s investment in
research and new product development increased 18%.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">For the fourth quarter of fiscal 2004, net
income increased 22% to $344,000, or $0.04 per diluted share, representing an
11% return on sales. This compared with net income of $282,000, or $0.04 per
diluted share, a year ago. IOMED has reported net income for eight consecutive
quarters.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<h4 style="font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Fiscal 2004 Operating Results</font></b></h4>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">For the fiscal year ended June 30, 2004,
IOMED reported sales of $12,189,000 compared with $11,935,000 a year ago. Gross
margin was 62% compared with 63% a year ago. The decrease in gross profit
margin was due, in part, to dealer price incentives introduced during the
fourth quarter. Selling, general and administrative expenses decreased 2%,
while research and development expenses decreased 15%. For fiscal 2004, IOMED
reported net income of $954,000, or $0.12 per diluted share, a 22% increase
over net income of $785,000, or $0.10 per diluted share, for the prior year.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">IOMED generated operating cash flow of $1.4
million for fiscal 2004. Working capital increased to $8.2 million and
shareholders&#146; equity reached $9.4 million. Total cash as of June 30, 2004 was
$8.4 million.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;We are pleased to have reported net income
over the last eight consecutive quarters, while generating over $3.1 million of
operating cash flow during that period. During fiscal 2004, we</font></p>
