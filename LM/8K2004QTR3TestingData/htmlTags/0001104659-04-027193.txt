<h1 style="color:windowtext;font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;page-break-after:avoid;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Angeion Corporation Reports 13.5% Third Quarter
Revenue Increase</font></b></h1>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;font-family:Times New Roman;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">SAINT PAUL, Minn. (September 7,
2004) &#151;</font></b><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;"> Angeion
Corporation (Nasdaq SC: ANGN) today reported results for its third quarter
ended July 31, 2004.&#160; Total revenue
increased by 13.5 percent to $5.2 million for the three months ended July 31,
2004 compared to $4.6 million for the same period in 2003.&#160; The net loss for the three months ended July
31, 2004 was $488,000, or $0.14 per share, compared to a net loss of $681,000,
or $0.19 per share, for the three months ended July 31, 2003.</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">For the nine months ended July 31, total revenue
increased 6.8 percent to $14.7 million in 2004 from $13.7 million in 2003.&#160; The net loss for the nine months ended July
31, 2004 was $2.1 million, or $0.58 per share, compared to a net loss of $1.9
million, or $0.54 per share, for the nine months ended July 31, 2003.&#160; The net loss for the nine months ended July
31, 2004 and 2003 included losses from discontinued operations of $350,000 and
$50,000, respectively.&#160; These losses from
discontinued operations included costs associated with the Company&#146;s purchase
of an additional year of product liability insurance together with other
expenses related to the Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (&#147;ICDs&#148;) that
were manufactured by the Company prior to 2000.</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Richard Jahnke, President and Chief Executive Officer
of the Company, stated, &#147;This is the sixth quarter of the past seven with
revenue increases over the comparable prior-year period.&#160; Of the past seven quarters, only the quarter
ended January 31, 2004, showed a decline from the prior year, which we believe
was attributable to the impact of our change in fiscal years on our sales
incentive plan.&#148;</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;We are particularly pleased that the revenue
increases are the result of improved performance in all aspects of our
business,&#148; continued Jahnke, &#147;We are continuing to see increased demand both
domestically and internationally for our cardiorespiratory diagnostic systems
including our new CPX Ultima&#153; gas exchange metabolic cart system that was
introduced at the end of first quarter.&#160;
We are also continuing to see increased market acceptance of our New
Leaf&#174; health and fitness products, which is resulting in both an increase in
the number of fitness clubs and training studios using the system and an
increase in the sales of assessment kits to consumers.&#148;</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; Detailed
discussions of the Company&#146;s financial position and results of operations are
contained in the Company&#146;s Form 10-KSB for the year ended October 31, 2003, and
the Forms 10-QSB for the quarters ended January 31, 2004, April 30, 2004 and
July 31, 2004, which have been filed with the SEC.</font></p>
