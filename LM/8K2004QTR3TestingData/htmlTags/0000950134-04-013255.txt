<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Docucorp Announces Fourth Quarter and Year-end Financial Results</B>



<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><B><I>Company Reports Record Earnings Per Share for the Year</I></B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>DALLAS &#151; September&nbsp;2, 2004 &#151; </B>Docucorp&#174; International (Nasdaq: DOCC), a leading
provider of enterprise information solutions, today announced record revenues
of $75.7&nbsp;million for fiscal year 2004, compared to revenues of $74.9&nbsp;million
for fiscal year 2003. The company reported net income of $5.4&nbsp;million, or
$0.47 per diluted share, for fiscal year 2004, compared to net income of $3.9
million, or $0.28 per diluted share, for the same period a year ago.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For the quarter ended July&nbsp;31, 2004, the company reported revenues of
$18.6&nbsp;million, compared to $19.4&nbsp;million for the same period a year ago. Net
income for the fourth quarter was $1.6&nbsp;million, or $0.14 per diluted share,
compared to net income of $1.1&nbsp;million, or $0.09 per diluted share, for the
fourth quarter of fiscal year 2003.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For the year, Docucorp&#146;s software license revenue rose 25&nbsp;percent from
$8.4&nbsp;million in fiscal year 2003 to $10.5&nbsp;million in fiscal year 2004.
Maintenance revenue increased four percent from $20.4&nbsp;million in fiscal year
2003 to $21.2&nbsp;million in fiscal year 2004, and ASP hosting revenue increased
two percent from $22.9&nbsp;million in fiscal year 2003 to $23.3&nbsp;million in fiscal
year 2004. Professional services revenue was $20.6&nbsp;million, down 11&nbsp;percent
compared to $23.3&nbsp;million for fiscal year 2003.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&#147;We are pleased with the double-digit software revenue growth and record
earnings per share for the fiscal year, especially in light of the current
business environment,&#148; said Michael

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>About Docucorp</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Docucorp is the authority in providing dynamic solutions for acquiring,
managing, personalizing
and presenting enterprise information. Servicing the entire enterprise
information lifecycle, Docucorp&#146;s information software, application service
provider (ASP)&nbsp;hosting and
professional consulting services enable companies to implement solutions
in-house or fully
outsource to Docucorp. The company has an installed base of more than 1,200
customers, including many of the largest insurance, utility and financial
services organizations. Headquartered in Dallas, Docucorp has facilities in
Atlanta GA, Silver Spring MD, Bedford NH, Eindhoven, Netherlands, Brussels,
Belgium and London.
