<h4 style="color:windowtext;font-style:italic;font-weight:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:normal;font-weight:bold;">Unify Reports First Quarter Fiscal 2005 Results</font></i></b></h4>

<h4 style="color:windowtext;font-style:italic;font-weight:normal;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:normal;">&nbsp;</font></i></h4>

<p style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">SACRAMENTO, Calif., </font></b>&#150;<b><font style="font-weight:bold;"> August&nbsp;24, 2004</font></b> &#150; Unify
Corp. (OTC BB: UNFY) today announced financial results for its first quarter of
fiscal 2005 ended July&nbsp;31, 2004.</p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Total revenues in the
first quarter were $2.7 million, a 17 percent decrease, compared to $3.3
million in the first quarter of fiscal 2004.&#160;
Software licenses revenue was $1.2 million, a decrease of 37 percent,
compared to $1.8 million for the fiscal 2004 first quarter.&#160; Net loss was $474,000 or $0.02 loss per
basic share, compared to a $144,000 or $0.01 loss in the first quarter of the
prior year.</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Unify ended the first
quarter with total cash and cash equivalents of $6.0 million and stockholders&#146;
equity of $4.1 million.</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="color:windowtext;font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;The revenue decline was
due to a reduction in Unify&#146;s application development tools and database
business, while Unify NXJ continued to gain traction,&#148; said Todd Wille, </font>president and CEO of Unify.&#160; &#147;During the quarter we saw a mix of
new customers, repeat business, and accelerating distribution channels with our
ISV partners and our Japan territory, as our customers discover that the Unify
NXJ platform is ten times faster for delivering applications that solve
critical business problems compared to competing alternatives.&#148;</p>

<p style="color:red;font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" color="black" face="Times New Roman" style="color:windowtext;font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" color="black" style="color:windowtext;font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><font color="black" style="color:windowtext;">Administrative Systems Inc. selected
