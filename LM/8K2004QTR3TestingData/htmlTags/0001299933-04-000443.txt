<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Dallas, TX. Collegiate Pacific (AMEX &#150; BOO) today reported record earnings for its fourth quarter
and year ended June&nbsp;30, 2004.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">Michael J. Blumenfeld, CEO stated: &#147;We are pleased to report record results for the quarter and
fiscal year ended June&nbsp;30,2004. FY04 results exceeded previously disclosed company expectations in
net sales ($39.6M vs. $38.0M); net income ($1.9M vs. $1.8M); and net income per share (&#147;EPS&#148;)
($0.25 vs. $0.24). When comparing FY04 net income and EPS to the previous year, it is important to
note that FY04 was fully taxed, whereas FY03 was not only non-taxed, but included a $350,000 tax
credit. As we move forward into FY05 (beginning July&nbsp;1, 2004), given the dynamic changes in the
Company, we encourage investors to use net sales, operating profit, net income and EPS as the key
metrics for evaluation. Metrics such as gross margins and SG&#038;A as a percentage of sales will be
less meaningful for comparative purposes with Tomark Sports, Kesslers Team Sports and Dixie
Sporting Goods being included in our results of operations for FY05.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">&#147;Achieving this level of performance while simultaneously bringing Tomark, Kesslers and now
Dixie onto our operating platform is gratifying, and a testament to the dedicated and hard working
employee base at Collegiate Pacific. Having successfully concluded a national training seminar in
late July for each of the acquired sales forces, we enter FY05 with high expectations. In addition
to maximizing the performance of these acquisitions and our organic business, we will continue to
seek strategic partners on the distribution, and/or manufacturing front as opportunities warrant.
In particular, we seek those partners who can influence the geographic reach of our sales force, or
those with products and brands that can add to the &#145;tool kit&#146; of our sales teams and catalogs. &#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">&#147;For fiscal 2005, we anticipate net sales to exceed $90.0M, net income of approximately $5.0M or
greater, and EPS of approximately $0.50. The Company may incur additional non-cash amortization
charges in conjunction with the acquisitions of Kesslers Team Sports and Dixie Sporting Goods
during FY05, however, these charges have been included in our EPS outlook.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 12pt">A conference call to discuss this earnings release and future plans is scheduled for today at 3:15
p.m. CDT. Interested parties may participate on the call by dialing in at least five minutes prior
to the start time of 3:15 p.m. CDT. The dial in number is 888-396-2384, participant passcode
28112209.
