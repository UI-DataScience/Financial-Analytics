<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-family:Times New Roman;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Burlingame, CA, September&nbsp;15,&nbsp;2004</font></b><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;"> - Valentis, Inc. (NASDAQ:VLTS)
today announced fiscal&nbsp;2004 financial results for the fourth quarter and
fiscal year ended June&nbsp;30,&nbsp;2004.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Valentis reported a net loss applicable to common stockholders for the
year ended June&nbsp;30,&nbsp;2004 of $6.5&nbsp;million, or
$0.81&nbsp;per&nbsp;basic and diluted share, on revenue of $7.5&nbsp;million,
compared to a net loss applicable to common stockholders of $43.0&nbsp;million,
or $13.86&nbsp;per&nbsp;basic and diluted share on revenue of $4.0&nbsp;million
for fiscal&nbsp;2003.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">For the quarter ended June&nbsp;30,&nbsp;2004, Valentis recorded a net
loss applicable to common stockholders of $3.7&nbsp;million, or $0.33 per basic
and diluted share, on revenue of $40,000, compared to a net loss applicable to
common shareholders of $2.1&nbsp;million, or $0.37 per basic and diluted share,
on revenue of $1.3&nbsp;million, for the quarter ended June&nbsp;30,&nbsp;2003.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p align="left" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On
June&nbsp;30,&nbsp;2004, Valentis had $20.5&nbsp;million in cash, cash
equivalents and short-term investments compared to $11.9&nbsp;million on
March&nbsp;31,&nbsp;2004 and $3.3&nbsp;million on June&nbsp;30,&nbsp;2003. The
increase of $8.6&nbsp;million and $17.2&nbsp;million in cash, cash equivalents
and short-term investments balances for the quarter and the year, respectively,
reflect a $6.5&nbsp;million payment from</font><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">

the settlement of a lawsuit in July&nbsp;2003, a $10&nbsp;million financing
completed in January&nbsp;2004 and a $12&nbsp;million financing completed in
June&nbsp;2004 offset by spending to fund the company&#146;s operations.</font></p>

<p align="left" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Research and development expenses for the fiscal year ended
June&nbsp;30,&nbsp;2004, remained at the same level on an annual basis as
fiscal&nbsp;2003 at $10.0&nbsp;million. For the fourth quarter ended
June&nbsp;30,&nbsp;2004, research and development expenses increased to
$2.6&nbsp;million from $2.2&nbsp;million in the corresponding period in
fiscal&nbsp;2003. The increases were attributable primarily to increased
clinical trial expenses for the Phase&nbsp;II Deltavasc&#153; study, which was
initiated in July&nbsp;2003, and were partially offset, by decreased expenses
resulting from staff reductions in October&nbsp;2002.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="font-family:Times New Roman;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;letter-spacing:-.1pt;">General
and administrative expenses </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">for the fiscal year ended June&nbsp;30,&nbsp;2004, <font style="letter-spacing:-.1pt;">decreased to $3.9&nbsp;million from
$8.9&nbsp;million in fiscal&nbsp;2003. General and administrative expenses </font>for
the quarter ended June&nbsp;30,&nbsp;2004, decreased to $1.1&nbsp;million from
$1.6&nbsp;million in the corresponding period in fiscal&nbsp;2003.<font style="letter-spacing:-.1pt;"> The decreases were attributable primarily to
decreased expenses for professional services resulting from the settlement of a
patent infringement litigation and staff reductions in October&nbsp;2002.</font></font></p>

dividend and deemed dividend charges of approximately $5.9&nbsp;million related
to Series&nbsp;A preferred stock and an adjustment of approximately
$22.3&nbsp;million resulting from the reduction in the Series&nbsp;A preferred
stock conversion price. These charges and the adjustment were included in the
net loss applicable to common stockholders for the fiscal year ended
June&nbsp;30,&nbsp;2003.</font></p>
