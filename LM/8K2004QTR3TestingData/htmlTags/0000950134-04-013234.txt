<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Sabine Royalty Trust</B>



<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>SABINE ROYALTY TRUST DECLARES<BR>
MONTHLY CASH DISTRIBUTION FOR SEPTEMBER</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>Dallas, Texas, September&nbsp;3, 2004 </B>&#150; Bank of America, N.A., Dallas, Texas,
as Trustee of the Sabine Royalty Trust (NYSE &#150; SBR), today declared a cash
distribution to the holders of its units of beneficial interest of $0.29537 per
unit, payable on September&nbsp;29, 2004, to unit holders of record on September&nbsp;15,
2004.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Due to the timing of the end of the month of August, approximately
$210,000 of revenue received will be posted in the following month of September
in addition to normal receipts during September.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;This distribution reflects primarily the oil production for June&nbsp;2004 and
the gas production for May&nbsp;2004. Preliminary production volumes are
approximately 50,000 barrels of oil and 517,000 mcf of gas.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For more information, including the latest financial reports on Sabine
Royalty Trust, please visit our website at<BR> http://www.sbr-sabineroyalty.com/.
