<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">EnerSys Reports Earnings for First Quarter
FY2005</font></b></p>

<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Reading, PA, September&nbsp;9, 2004, EnerSys (NYSE: ENS), one of the
world&#146;s largest manufacturers, marketers and distributors of industrial
batteries, today announced net earnings for its first fiscal quarter of 2005
ending July&nbsp;4, 2004 of $9,027,000 and net earnings available to common
shareholders of $872,000, or $0.08 per share (basic and diluted) compared to
$5,800,000 of net earnings and $157,000 of net earnings available to common
shareholders or $0.01 per share (basic and diluted) in the prior year first
fiscal quarter.&#160; After giving effect to
the July&nbsp;30, 2004 EnerSys IPO as if it occurred at the beginning of the
first quarter of both fiscal 2005 and 2004, pro forma net earnings per share
were $0.23 per share (basic and diluted) in the fiscal 2005 first quarter and
$0.11 per share basic and $0.10 per share diluted in the fiscal 2004 first
quarter.&#160; Net sales for the first
quarter were $263,261,000 compared to $218,265,000 in the prior year first
quarter, or an increase of over 20%, with net earnings up over 50% from the
prior year and diluted pro forma net earnings per share up 130% from the first
quarter of the prior year results.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">EnerSys&#146; fiscal 2005 first quarter operating results for its reporting
segments compared to the fiscal 2004 first quarter are as follows:</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Reserve Power &#150; Net sales increased over 16% to $124,800,000, while
operating earnings increased over 175% to $11,300,000 with margins increasing
530 bps to 9.1%.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt .5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Motive Power &#150; Net sales increased over 24% to $138,500,000, while
operating earnings increased over 30% to $11,300,000 with margins increasing 40
bps to 8.2%.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;We achieved a strong improvement in our first quarter of fiscal 2005
net sales and earnings results in our global business primarily due to three
factors.&#160; First is the positive impact
from the first synchronized global economic recovery in many years.&#160; Second is significant growth in our Asian
business with net sales increasing over 67%.&#160;
Third is our success in continuing to expand our global market share,&#148;
stated John D. Craig, Chairman, President and Chief Executive Officer of the
company.&#160; &#147;We anticipate in the fiscal
2005 second quarter, traditionally our slowest, pro forma net earnings per
share will be comparable to those of our first quarter,&#148; he said.</font></p>
