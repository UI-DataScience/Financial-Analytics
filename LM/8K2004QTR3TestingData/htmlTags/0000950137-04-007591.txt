<P align="center" style="font-size: 14pt"><B><U>QUIKSILVER, INC. REPORTS RECORD 2004 THIRD QUARTER </U><BR>
<U>OPERATING RESULTS</U></B>



<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt">&#151; Consolidated Revenues Increase 34% &#151;<BR>
&#151; Earnings Per Share increase 52% to $0.32 &#151;



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;HUNTINGTON BEACH, CALIFORNIA, SEPTEMBER 9, 2004 &#151; Quiksilver, Inc.
(NYSE:ZQK) today announced record operating results for the third quarter ended
July&nbsp;31, 2004.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Consolidated revenues for the third quarter of fiscal 2004 increased 34%
to $337.9&nbsp;million as compared to fiscal 2003 third quarter consolidated
revenues of $251.5&nbsp;million. Consolidated net income for the third quarter of
fiscal 2004 increased 64% to $19.5&nbsp;million as compared to $11.9&nbsp;million. Third
quarter fully diluted earnings per share was $0.32 versus $0.21 for the third
quarter of fiscal 2003.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Robert B. McKnight, Jr., Chairman of the Board and Chief Executive Officer
of Quiksilver, Inc., commented, &#147;We are very pleased with our accomplishments
during the third quarter. This represents our 11th consecutive quarter of
exceeding expectations and further highlights our excellent track record of
generating strong and consistent financial results. Our business trends are
solid, and we are encouraged about our prospects for the future.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Revenues in the Americas increased 37% during the third quarter of fiscal
2004 to $187.9&nbsp;million as compared to fiscal 2003 third quarter revenues of
$137.4&nbsp;million. As measured in U.S. dollars and reported in the financial
statements, European revenues increased 23% during the third quarter of fiscal
2004 to $115.4&nbsp;million as compared to fiscal 2003 third quarter European
revenues of $93.9&nbsp;million. As measured in euros, European revenues increased
17% for those same periods. Asia/Pacific revenues increased 67% to $33.1
million in the third quarter of fiscal 2004 compared to $19.8&nbsp;million in the
third quarter of 2003. As measured in Australian dollars, Asia/Pacific
revenues increased 56%.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Mr.&nbsp;McKnight continued, &#147;The feedback regarding our Spring lines at recent
trade shows around the world, including MAGIC in the U.S. and ASR in Europe,
was very positive and reflects our ongoing commitment to develop the best
product in the market. We believe that our merchandise is trend right and
underscores our status as the dominant lifestyle company for kids and young
adults around the world.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Company also confirmed its expectations for the fourth quarter fiscal
ending October&nbsp;31, 2004 that revenues will range between $315&nbsp;million and $320
million, and diluted earnings per share will range between $0.36 and $0.37.
For the full fiscal year, revenues are expected to range between $1.23&nbsp;billion
and $1.24&nbsp;billion with diluted earnings per share ranging from $1.31 to $1.32.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Consolidated inventories increased 8% to $171.6&nbsp;million at July&nbsp;31, 2004
from $159.5&nbsp;million at July&nbsp;31, 2003. Consolidated trade accounts receivable
increased 25% to $271.4&nbsp;million at July&nbsp;31, 2004 from $217.9&nbsp;million at July
31, 2003. Average days sales outstanding decreased by approximately four days
compared to the third quarter of the prior year, while inventories grew 5% in
constant dollars.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&#147;This first quarter with DC Shoes included in our results was truly
exceptional. Congratulations to all of our people around the world not only for
the quarterly results, but for the tremendous spirit in which we&#146;ve integrated
DC into our company,&#148; Bernard Mariette, President of Quiksilver, Inc.,
commented. &#147;We&#146;re very impressed by the way that Ken Block, Damon Way and
their DC team are already contributing to the development of Quiksilver
footwear. We can see the synergies of working together happening faster than
we could have expected.&#148;


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Mr.&nbsp;McKnight concluded, &#147;Our continued success is really a function of
continuing to adhere to a few key disciplines: great product; careful brand
management; a healthy philosophy regarding growth; and a strategic development
plan designed to enhance each part of our business. Also fundamental to our
consistency is the diversification of our company and the continuity of our
management teams. We remain focused on further leveraging our leadership
position in the industry and fully capitalizing on the many opportunities that
lie ahead.&#148;

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">About Quiksilver:



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Quiksilver designs, produces and distributes clothing, accessories and
related products for young-minded people and develops brands that represent a
casual lifestyle&#150;driven from a boardriding heritage. Quiksilver&#146;s authenticity
is evident in its innovative products, events and retail environments across
the globe.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Quiksilver&#146;s primary focus is apparel, footwear and related accessories
for young men and young women under the Quiksilver, Roxy, DC Shoes, Raisins,
and Radio Fiji labels. Quiksilver also manufactures apparel, footwear and
related accessories for boys (Quiksilver Boys and Hawk Clothing), girls (Roxy
Girl, Teenie Wahine and Raisins Girls), men (Quiksilveredition and Fidra) and
women (Leilani swimwear), as well as snowboards, snowboard boots and bindings
under the Lib Technologies, Gnu, DC Shoes, Roxy and Bent Metal labels.
Quiksilver&#146;s products are sold throughout the world, primarily in surf shops,
skate shops and other specialty stores that provide an authentic retail
experience for our customers.
