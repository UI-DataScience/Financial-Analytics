 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="right"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>EXHIBIT 99.1 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT
SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>THE ROWE COMPANIES ANNOUNCES </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>THIRD
QUARTER OUTLOOK </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">McLean, VA, September 15, 2004&#150; The Rowe Companies
(AMEX:ROW), a leading furniture manufacturer and home furnishings retailer, announced today that, while sales volume exceeded expectations, net earnings for its third fiscal quarter ended August 29, 2004 will be lower than anticipated due to
manufacturing productivity below expected levels. This manufacturing inefficiency is associated with time requirements for the implementation of its new Enterprise Resource Planning (&#147;ERP&#148;) system and the conversion to lean manufacturing.
</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;While it is not The Rowe Company&#146;s policy to give
specific guidance, I am making an exception in the interest of good financial communication&#148;, said Gerald Birnbach, Chairman and President. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Net shipments are expected to be approximately $76.5 million, or an increase of approximately 9% over the same quarter last year. The Company, however,
expects to report net earnings for the quarter below analyst estimates. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman"
SIZE="2">&#147;We continue to be pleased with Storehouse retail demand and Rowe Furniture manufacturing orders&#148;, Mr. Birnbach continued. &#147;Operations management in the Rowe Furniture division has been intensely focused on the implementation
of our new ERP system and conversion to lean manufacturing, significantly reducing the time available for day-to-day management activities. During this period manufacturing productivity has declined below our expected levels. While it is anticipated
that the line management time requirement for conversion to the new system should diminish in the near future at which time our line managers will return to their normal responsibilities, we will not make the conversion until we are satisfied that
the system will operate as planned&#148;. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">&#147;Our new ERP
system will give us substantial new capabilities in critical areas such as merchandising, and gives us the scale for growth. While we are disappointed that our earnings aren&#146;t higher, we know that this kind of a disruption is temporary and not
uncommon when undertaking complex technology initiatives&#148; Mr. Birnbach continued. </FONT></P>

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">The Rowe Companies anticipates releasing earnings for the third quarter on September 22nd followed by a

in the home furnishings industry: Rowe Furniture, Inc., a major manufacturer of quality upholstered furniture serving the middle and upper middle market throughout the U.S.; and Storehouse, Inc., a multi-channel, lifestyle home furnishings business
including 60 retail home furnishings stores. Storehouse makes good design accessible by selling an edited assortment of casual, contemporary home furnishings through its stores located in the Southeast, Southwest and Mid-Atlantic markets, its
