<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">BED BATH &amp; BEYOND INC. REPORTS NET EARNINGS
INCREASE OF 23.5% IN FISCAL SECOND QUARTER</font></b></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt 3.25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><b><font style="font-weight:bold;">Net Earnings Per Share
of&#160; $.39 for Quarter</font></b></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt 3.25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><b><font style="font-weight:bold;">Quarterly Net Sales Increase
by 14.6%</font></b></p>

<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt 3.25in;text-indent:-.25in;"><font size="3" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:12.0pt;">&#149;</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><b><font style="font-weight:bold;">Comparable Quarterly Store
Sales Increase by 4.8%</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">UNION,
New Jersey, September&nbsp;22, 2004 &#151; <b><font style="font-weight:bold;">Bed
Bath &amp; Beyond Inc.</font></b> today reported net earnings of $120.0 million
($.39 per share) in the fiscal second quarter ended August&nbsp;28, 2004, an
increase of approximately 23.5% from the $97.2 million ($.32 per share) earned
in the fiscal second quarter of 2003.&#160;
Net sales for the fiscal second quarter of 2004 were approximately $1.3
billion, an increase of approximately 14.6% from net sales of approximately
$1.1 billion in the fiscal second quarter of 2003.&#160; Comparable store sales in the fiscal second quarter of 2004 grew
by approximately 4.8%, compared with an increase of approximately 5.9% in last
year&#146;s fiscal second quarter.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">For
the fiscal first half ended August&nbsp;28, 2004, net earnings increased 30.6%
to $202.1 million ($.66 per share) from $154.7 million ($.51 per share) earned
in the comparable period of the prior year.&#160;
Net sales for the fiscal first half of 2004 rose 18.4% to approximately
$2.4 billion from approximately $2.0 billion in the corresponding period of the
prior year. &#160;Comparable store sales for
the fiscal first half of 2004 increased by approximately 4.9%, compared with an
increase of approximately 5.2% in the comparable period a year ago.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">As
of August&nbsp;28, 2004, the Company operated a total of 661 stores, including
606 <b><font style="font-weight:bold;">Bed Bath &amp;</font></b>  <b><font style="font-weight:bold;">Beyond</font></b> stores (of which 14 were opened
during the fiscal second quarter) in 44 states and Puerto Rico.&#160; In addition, as of that date, <b><font style="font-weight:bold;">Christmas Tree Shops, Inc.</font></b> (acquired in
fiscal 2003) operated 24 stores in 6 states, and <b><font style="font-weight:bold;">Harmon Stores, Inc.</font></b> (acquired in fiscal 2002) operated 31
stores in 3 states.&#160; Consolidated total
square feet of store space as of August&nbsp;28, 2004 was approximately 21.3
million square feet.</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">During
the remainder of fiscal 2004 (which ends on February&nbsp;26, 2005) the Company
plans to open approximately 54 <b><font style="font-weight:bold;">Bed Bath &amp;
Beyond</font></b> stores, in both new and existing markets, 2 <b><font style="font-weight:bold;">Christmas Tree Shops</font></b> and approximately 4 <b><font style="font-weight:bold;">Harmon Stores</font></b>.</font></p>
