<P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>ROANOKE, VA, September 8, 2004 &#151; </B>Optical Cable Corporation (Nasdaq NM: OCCF) today announced financial results for its fiscal third quarter and nine months
