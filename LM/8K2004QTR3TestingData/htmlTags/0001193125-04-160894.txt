 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Item 2.02. Results of Operations and Financial Condition. </B></FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">As more fully discussed below in Item 4.02, on September 22, 2004, Dynegy Inc.
(&#147;Dynegy&#148;), the wholly owning parent of Dynegy Holdings Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;), issued a press release announcing that the previously issued financial statements contained in Dynegy&#146;s 2003 Form 10-K and first and second
quarter 2004 Form 10-Qs should not be relied upon because of errors in those financial statements and that those financial statements would be restated to make the necessary accounting adjustments. </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">As a result of the adjustments identified in the Dynegy press release relating to
Dynegy&#146;s deferred income tax accounts, which the Company expects will similarly impact the Company&#146;s deferred income tax accounts, the previously issued financial statements contained in the Company&#146;s 2003 Form 10-K and first and
second quarter 2004 Form 10-Qs should also not be relied upon and will also be restated to make the necessary accounting adjustments. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>Item 4.02 Non-Reliance on Previously Issued Financial Statements or a Related Audit Report or Completed Interim Review. </B></FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">On September 22, 2004, Dynegy issued a press release announcing that, on September 21, 2004,
the Audit and Compliance Committee of Dynegy&#146;s Board of Directors (&#147;Audit Committee&#148;) concluded that the previously issued financial statements contained in Dynegy&#146;s 2003 Form 10-K and first and second quarter 2004 Form 10-Qs
should not be relied upon because of errors in those financial statements and that Dynegy would restate those financial statements to make the necessary accounting adjustments. The Dynegy restatements relate to (i) Dynegy&#146;s previously disclosed
goodwill impairment charge associated with the pending sale of Illinois Power Company (&#147;Illinois Power&#148;) and (ii) Dynegy&#146;s deferred income tax accounts. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Although the Company&#146;s financial statements are not impacted by the additional impairment charges to be recorded by Dynegy in
connection with the Illinois Power sale, as a result of Dynegy&#146;s income tax review initiative, the Company has also determined that adjustments related to its deferred income tax accounts in periods prior to 2004 are required. As this deferred
income tax review initiative has not been completed, the Company is currently unable to determine with certainty the amount of the adjustments and the periods affected. However, the Company does anticipate, based on the ongoing review of these tax
items, that these adjustments will reduce the Company&#146;s deferred tax liability reflected on its balance sheet. Dynegy is working to complete this review as expeditiously as possible. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Based upon these matters, the Company expects to file amendments to its 2003 Form 10-K and first and second quarter 2004 Form 10-Qs,
reflecting all necessary deferred tax adjustments, prior to filing its third quarter 2004 Form 10-Q in November 2004. Accordingly, the referenced financial statements should not be relied upon until such time as the Company files its restatements.
