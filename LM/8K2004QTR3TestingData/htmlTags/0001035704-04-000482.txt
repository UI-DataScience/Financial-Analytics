<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>CANYON RESOURCES UPDATES STATUS OF DELAYED<BR>
SECOND QUARTER REPORT ON FORM 10-Q</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>GOLDEN, CO&#151;Canyon Resources Corporation (AMEX:CAU), </B>a Colorado-based
mining company, previously announced that it would not timely file its Second
Quarter Report on Form 10-Q. The Company is continuing to review the
appropriateness under generally accepted accounting principles of its
impairment model for evaluating the Briggs Mine assets for possible impairment
under SFAS No.&nbsp;144 &#147;Accounting for the Impairment or Disposal of Long-Lived
Assets&#148;. The Company estimates it will complete this process, and if necessary,
test the Briggs Mine assets for possible impairment and then file its Second
Quarter Report within the next two weeks.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;If the Briggs Mine assets are impaired, the likely impairment would have
occurred in prior years and is not likely to affect the results of the current
quarter. Although the following financial information is subject to
finalization, the Company expects a net loss of $2.3&nbsp;million or $0.08 per share
for the three months ended June&nbsp;30, 2004 compared to a restated* net loss of
$3.0&nbsp;million or $0.14 per share for the comparable period of 2003. Revenues
during the second quarter of 2004 were $5.8&nbsp;million compared to $3.4&nbsp;million
for the same period of 2003.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For the three months ended June&nbsp;30, 2004, the Company sold 14,805 ounces
of gold at an average price of $394 per equivalent gold ounce. For the
comparable period of 2003, the Company sold 9,681 ounces of gold and 4,043
ounces of silver at an average realized price of $350 per equivalent gold
ounce. The New York Commodity Exchange (COMEX)&nbsp;gold price averaged $393 and
$347 per ounce for the three months ended June&nbsp;30, 2004 and 2003, respectively.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The Company had cash and cash equivalents of $9.1&nbsp;million at June&nbsp;30,
2004.
