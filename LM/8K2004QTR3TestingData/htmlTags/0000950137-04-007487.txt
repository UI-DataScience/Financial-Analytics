<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Stratos International Announces First Quarter Results</B>



<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Thursday, September&nbsp;2, 2004, 4:02 pm ET


<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>Reports Q1 Sales of $21 Million</B>


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">CHICAGO, Sept. 2 /PRNewswire-FirstCall/ &#151; Stratos International, Inc. (Nasdaq:
STLW), a leading provider of optoelectronic, fiber optic, and radio frequency
(RF)&nbsp;and microwave subsystems and components, today announced financial results
for its first quarter ended July&nbsp;31, 2004.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Sales for the first quarter of fiscal 2005 were $21.0&nbsp;million. The Company also
recorded license fees and royalty income of $2.8&nbsp;million in the first quarter
of fiscal 2005, which included a $2.5&nbsp;million patent infringement settlement
received in July&nbsp;2004.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">For comparison, fourth quarter fiscal 2004 revenue for the three months ended
April&nbsp;30, 2004 was $19.7&nbsp;million. Fourth quarter fiscal 2004 license fees and
royalty income for the three months ended April&nbsp;30, 2004 was $428,000.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Jim McGinley, CEO of Stratos, remarked, &#147;We are pleased to report our fourth
consecutive quarter of revenue growth. Continued growth in our optoelectronic
and fiber optic products combined with the acquisition of the RF Microwave
business has provided significant sales gains from a year ago. Our balance
sheet remains healthy with approximately $37&nbsp;million of cash and short-term
investments at quarter end.&#148;

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The net loss attributable to common shareholders for the first quarter of
fiscal 2005 was $480,000, or $0.04 per share. By comparison, the Company
reported a net loss attributable to common shareholders of $7.3&nbsp;million or
$0.99 per share for the first quarter of fiscal 2004, and a net loss
attributable to common shareholders of $7.0&nbsp;million or $0.52 per share for the
fourth quarter of fiscal 2004.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The Company completed the acquisition of Sterling Holding Company, a leading
designer, manufacturer and marketer of specialty RF and microwave components,
on November&nbsp;6, 2003 as previously announced. Financial results include
Sterling&#146;s operating results from that date. The merger with Sterling provided
Stratos with two leading brands, Trompeter and Semflex, a suite of highly
profitable and complementary products, an expanded customer base and strong
sales channels to further its penetration into the military and video markets.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Company Exploring Strategic Alternatives

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">In May, Stratos International announced its decision to explore various
strategic alternatives to maximize shareholder value, including the possible
sale of the Company. The Company has retained CIBC World Markets Corp. as its
exclusive financial advisor. There is no assurance that a transaction will
result involving the Company. In the meantime, the Company expects to continue
operations as usual by seeking new business, developing new products and
working with customers to maintain a high level of customer service.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Webcast/Conference Call

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Stratos International will host a live audio webcast and conference call on
Thursday, September&nbsp;2, 2004 at 5:00 pm EDT. Investors and other interested
parties may listen to the live webcast by visiting the investor relations
section of the Stratos International website at http://www.stratoslightwave.com. James

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">ABOUT STRATOS INTERNATIONAL

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Stratos International, Inc. is a leading designer, developer and manufacturer
of active and passive optical, optoelectronic, RF and Microwave components,
subsystems and interconnect products used in telecom, enterprise, military and
video markets.

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">Stratos has a rich history of optical and mechanical packaging expertise and
has been a pioneer in developing several optical devices using innovative form
factors for telecom, datacom and harsh environments application. This
expertise, coupled with several strategic acquisitions, has allowed the Company
to amass a broad range of products and build a strong IP portfolio of more than
100 patents. The Company is a market leader in several niches including high
margin specialty optical products such as RJ and low rider transceivers, Media
Interface Adapters, flex circuits, as well as high performance RF and microwave
coax and triax interconnect products. The Company currently serves more than
400 active customers in telecom, military and video markets.

and actual results may differ materially from any future performance suggested.
These factors include rapid technological change in the optical communications
