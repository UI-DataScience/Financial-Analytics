<P ALIGN=center><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>CUNO Reports
  Record Third Quarter Results </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Flush Level 1" FSL="Workstation" -->
<TABLE WIDTH=100% CELLPADDING=0 CELLSPACING=0>
<TR VALIGN=TOP>
<TD WIDTH=5%><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp; </FONT></TD>
    <TD WIDTH=95%><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2> * Record
      third quarter Sales of $89.9 million (up 20%)<br>
      * Record third quarter diluted EPS of $0.50 (up 14%)<br>
      * Record third quarter Orders of $90.1 million (up 19%) </FONT></TD>
</TR>
</TABLE>
<BR>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent 00" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;MERIDEN,
Conn., Aug. 25 /PRNewswire-FirstCall/ &#151; CUNO Incorporated (Nasdaq: CUNO) today
reported record third quarter results for the period ended July 31, 2004. Sales were $89.9
million, up 20% versus the same period in 2003 and up 17% in local currency. Net income
for the third quarter increased by 15% to $8.6 million from $7.5 million reported in the
same period in 2003. Diluted earnings per share for the period were a third quarter record
$0.50, up 14% compared to $0.44 in the prior year quarter and up approximately 9% on a
currency-adjusted basis. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent 00" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Commenting
on the Company&#146;s third quarter results, Mark G. Kachur, Chairman and Chief Executive
Officer, said &#147;Our record third quarter results reflect the strength and
effectiveness of our business model. We achieved double-digit sales growth in each of our
primary markets, including Potable Water, Healthcare and Fluid Processing. Our geographic
diversity also contributed to our strong overall results, as international sales increased
by 26% (up 20% in local currency), led by strong growth in Japan, Europe and the
Asia/Pacific region.&#148; </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent 00" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For
the first nine months of fiscal 2004, sales were a record $248.1 million, up 18% (up 12%
in local currency), and net income was $24.1 million versus $19.2 million in the prior
year period. Strong performance both in our international operations and in North America
drove the sales growth. Diluted earnings per share were a record $1.41, up 25% from $1.13
in the first nine months of 2003 and up approximately 15% on a currency-adjusted basis. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent 00" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Commenting
on business conditions and the outlook for CUNO, Mr. Kachur added, &#147;Our incoming
orders were a third quarter record $90.1 million and our backlog remains strong. As a
result, we expect to achieve continued strong financial results during the fourth quarter,
and we are comfortable with the current $1.86 consensus of analysts&#146; EPS estimates
for the full year.&#148; </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent 00" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;As
previously announced, on August 2, 2004, CUNO completed the acquisition of WTC Industries,
Inc. (&#147;WTC&#148;). The aggregate consideration for the outstanding WTC shares
(including payment for the settlement of outstanding stock options and warrants) was
approximately $115 million (including the assumption of WTC&#146;s outstanding bank debt
and all acquisition-related expenses). CUNO funded the acquisition with a combination of
existing cash and bank borrowings under a new variable-rate, 5-year, $120 million
unsecured revolving credit facility. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent 00" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Commenting
on this recently completed transaction, Mark G. Kachur, CUNO&#146;s Chairman and Chief
Executive Officer, said &#147;The acquisition of WTC Industries will broaden CUNO&#146;s
product line, technology portfolio and customer base and expand the Company&#146;s
presence in the water filtration business. WTC is an excellent strategic fit with our
Potable Water business, CUNO&#146;s largest and fastest growing business. Combining
CUNO&#146;s and WTC&#146;s capabilities and strengths in engineering, innovative product
development, customized manufacturing and highly focused customer support should enable us
to provide greater value to our customers and shareholders. With the addition of WTC, we
expect our worldwide OMA sales (i.e. sales to OEMs, multi-level marketers, and appliance
manufacturers) to exceed $100 million in fiscal 2005 and our future growth prospects in
the water filtration business will be significantly enhanced.&#148; </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent 00" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A
conference call will be held Thursday, August 26 at 10:00 a.m. (EST) to review the
Company&#146;s third quarter financial results and business outlook. The call-in number is
1-888-428-4479 for interested analysts and investors. Alternatively, visit us at
http://www.cuno.com for additional information about the Company. </FONT></P>

<!-- MARKER FORMAT-SHEET="Para Indent 00" FSL="Workstation" -->
<P><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif" SIZE=2>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;CUNO
is a world leader in the design, manufacture and marketing of a comprehensive line of
filtration products for the separation, clarification and purification of liquids and
gases. CUNO&#146;s products, which include proprietary depth filters and semi-permeable
membrane filters, are used in the potable water, healthcare, and fluid processing markets. </FONT></P>
