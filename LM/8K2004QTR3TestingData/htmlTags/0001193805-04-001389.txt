<P ALIGN="CENTER"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><FONT SIZE="2"><b>INSIGHTFUL
  ANNOUNCES OPERATING RESULTS FOR SECOND QUARTER 2004</b></FONT></FONT> </P>

<P ALIGN="CENTER"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><FONT SIZE="2"><b>Reports
  Profit and Growth</b></FONT></FONT> </P>

<P ALIGN="LEFT"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><FONT SIZE="2"><b>SEATTLE
  &#151; September 9, 2004&#151;</b> Insightful Corporation (NASDAQ: IFULE), a
  leading provider of software solutions for analysis of numeric and text data,
  today announced financial results for the second quarter 2004.<br>
  </FONT></FONT> </P>

<P ALIGN="LEFT"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><FONT SIZE="2">Insightful
reported revenues of $4.5 million for the second quarter of 2004, an increase of 3% over
both first  quarter 2004 and second quarter 2003 revenues of $4.3 million.  Insightful
reported net income of  $0.3 million  or $0.02 per share for the second quarter of 2004
compared to net income of $0.2 million or $0.02 per share in  the first quarter of 2004
and net loss of $(0.4) million or $(0.04) per share for the second quarter of 2003  which
included a $(0.1) million or $(0.01) per share loss from discontinued operations.<br></FONT></FONT> </P>

<P ALIGN="LEFT"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><FONT SIZE="2">The cash
balance at June 30, 2004 was $8.6 million compared to $7.2 million at March 31, 2004.<br></FONT></FONT> </P>

<P ALIGN="LEFT"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><FONT SIZE="2">&#147;We are
pleased with our second quarter results,&#148; said Jeff Coombs, president and CEO of
Insightful.  &#147;Our profit  and positive cash flow from operations was driven by a
resurgence in our professional services business.&#148;<br></FONT></FONT> </P>

<P ALIGN="LEFT"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><FONT SIZE="2">&#147;We showed
a more balanced revenue blend between software and services,&#148; Coombs continued.
&#147;While software  license revenues were relatively flat year-over-year--bucking our
recent trend of three consecutive quarters of  year-over-year double digit growth--we are
hopeful that increased training and consulting activity across a wider  number of
customers will fuel software license growth in coming quarters as we help our customers
develop and  deploy predictive analytics.&#148;<br></FONT></FONT> </P>

<P ALIGN="LEFT"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><FONT SIZE="2"><b>Domestic

<P ALIGN="LEFT"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><FONT SIZE="2">Total Data
Analysis revenues, including software licenses, maintenance and professional services and

first quarter of 2004 and the second  quarter of 2003.<br></FONT></FONT> </P>

<P ALIGN="LEFT"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><FONT SIZE="2"><b>Text
  Analysis</b><br>

<P ALIGN="LEFT"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><FONT SIZE="2">Total text
analysis revenues were $148,000 in the second quarter of 2004 compared to $164,000 in the
first  quarter of 2004 and $163,000 in the second quarter of 2003.<br></FONT></FONT> </P>

  Highlights:</b><br>

and positive cash flows from operations for three consecutive quarters.<br><br></font></li>

<li><font size="2" face="Times New Roman, Times, serif">Second quarter 2004 professional
services and other revenues increased to $1.0 million from $0.5 million in  the first
quarter of 2004 and $0.7 million in the second quarter of 2003.<br><br></font></li>



<li><font size="2" face="Times New Roman, Times, serif">Total international data analysis

<li><font size="2" face="Times New Roman, Times, serif">Total domestic data analysis
revenues were $2.8 million in the second quarter of 2004 compared to $2.8  million in the
first quarter of 2004 and $2.9 million in the second quarter of 2003.<br><br></font></li>

<li><font size="2" face="Times New Roman, Times, serif">Second quarter 2004 software
license revenues were $1.9 million, flat compared to $1.9 million generated  in the

of  2004.<br><br></font></li>

  <li><font size="2" face="Times New Roman, Times, serif">First half 2004 software

<P ALIGN="LEFT"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><FONT SIZE="2"><b>ABOUT

company is a leading supplier of  software and services for statistical data analysis,
data mining and knowledge access enabling clients to gain  intelligence from numeric and
text data.<br></FONT></FONT> </P>

  for the design, development and deployment of customized solutions.<br>
  </FONT></FONT> </P>

<P ALIGN="LEFT"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman, Times, Serif"><FONT SIZE="2">The company has
