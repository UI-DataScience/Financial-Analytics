<p align="left" style="font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">MAI Systems Reports Second Quarter 2004 Results</font></b></p>

<p align="left" style="font-weight:bold;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:left;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:normal;">&nbsp;</font></b></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">LAKE FOREST, CA (August&nbsp;24, 2004)</font></b><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">  </font><font size="2" style="font-size:10.0pt;">MAI Systems Corporation (OTCBB: MAIY.OB)
announced today that its second quarter 2004 revenue, net income (loss), net
income (loss) per share were $4.9 million, $0.1 million, and $0.01
respectively. This compares to $4.8 million, ($0.1) million, and ($0.01) for
the second quarter 2003. Revenue, net income, and income per share for the six
month period ended June&nbsp;30, 2004 were $10.1 million, $0.2 million, and
$0.02 respectively. This compares to $9.9 million, $0.2 million, and $0.01 for
the comparable period in 2003.</font></p>

provides total enterprise management solutions, improving critical operations
and profitability for global lodging organizations. For information on the
