<P align="center" style="font-size: 10pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>Harris Interactive Adjusts Fiscal 2004 Stockholder Equity Accounts</B>
<Br><I>Action Results in Change to After-tax Earnings, but No Change to Pre-tax
Earnings</I>

<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><B>ROCHESTER, NY &#151; September&nbsp;7, 2004 </B>&#151; Harris Interactive&#174; (Nasdaq:HPOL) announced
an adjustment to the previously reported income tax benefit for its fiscal
fourth quarter and full fiscal year, which ended on June&nbsp;30, 2004.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">During preparation of the Harris Interactive Form 10-K for the fiscal year
ended June&nbsp;30, 2004, the Company effected an adjustment of $1.9&nbsp;million between
two stockholders&#146; equity accounts. The adjustment impacts the income tax
benefit previously reported, as well as the net profit previously reported.
The adjustment does not change the Company&#146;s previously reported pre-tax income
or the amount of reported net operating loss carryforwards, or any tax asset or
liability account.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">With this change, the tax benefit from deductions attributable to the exercise
of non-qualified stock options and disqualifying dispositions of incentive
stock options are now reflected as a credit to additional paid-in capital
accounts.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">During fiscal 2004, $4.7&nbsp;million of such deductions generated a tax benefit of
$1.9&nbsp;million. In the Company&#146;s earlier press release, this $1.9&nbsp;million was
treated as a tax benefit in the income statement rather than a direct credit to
the additional paid-in capital account. As a result of the adjustment, the
actual tax benefit to be reported in our income statement for fiscal 2004 will
be $16.0&nbsp;million, or $0.28 per fully diluted share, rather than the $17.8
million and $0.31 per fully diluted share previously reported. In addition,
the actual net profit will be $29.9&nbsp;million, or $0.52 per fully diluted share,
rather than the $31.8&nbsp;million or $0.55 per fully diluted share previously
reported. In addition, as a result of the adjustment, our net income for the
fourth quarter was $24.8&nbsp;million, or $0.43 per fully diluted share, including a
tax benefit of $18.7&nbsp;million, rather than the previously reported net income of
$26.7&nbsp;million, or $0.46 per fully diluted share, including a tax benefit of
$20.6&nbsp;million.


<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt">The correction does not alter any of the Company&#146;s previously announced
guidance for fiscal 2005.
