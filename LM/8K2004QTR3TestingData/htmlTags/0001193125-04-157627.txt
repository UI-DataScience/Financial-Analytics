 <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="right"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>EXHIBIT 99 </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT
FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>VCAT Reports Results for the Fourth Quarter </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px" ALIGN="center"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>and Fiscal Year Ended June 30, 2004 </B></FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B>San Diego, California, September 16, 2004</B> &#150; Venture Catalyst
Incorporated (&#147;VCAT&#148;) (OTCBB: VCAT), today reported the operating results for the fourth quarter and fiscal year ended June 30, 2004. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2"><B><U>Fourth Quarter Results</U> </B></FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:-6px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">VCAT reported net income for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2004, of $3,725,000 or $0.52 per share. This compares with net income of $1,986,000, or $0.28
per share, for the fourth quarter of fiscal 2003. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Revenues for
the fourth quarter of fiscal 2004 were $1,738,000 as compared to $1,778,000 during the fourth quarter of fiscal 2003. Revenues from consulting services in the fourth quarter of fiscal 2004 were $1,725,000 compared to $1,776,000 earned during the
fourth quarter of fiscal 2003. Revenues from software products and services in the fourth quarter of fiscal 2004 were $12,000, as compared to $2,000 in the fourth quarter of fiscal 2003. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Cost of revenues increased to $1,173,000 during the fourth quarter of fiscal 2004 from $272,000 during the fourth quarter of
fiscal 2003. The anticipated contract loss related to the Consulting Agreement with the Barona Tribe (the &#147;Consulting Agreement&#148;) was reduced by $1,572,000 during the fourth quarter of fiscal 2003. </FONT></P> <P
STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Operating expenses, consisting of general and administrative, sales and
marketing and research and development expenses, decreased to $829,000 during the fourth quarter of fiscal 2004 from $856,000 during the fourth quarter of fiscal 2003. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT
SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">Net other income during the fourth quarter of fiscal 2004 was $3,450,000, resulting from a one-time gain of $3,714,000 from
the release of the liability referred to as &#147;advance of future consulting fees&#148;, partially offset by interest expense of $278,000 from VCAT&#146;s long-term debt. During the fourth quarter of fiscal 2003, net other expense was $237,000
resulting primarily from interest expense of $252,000 on VCAT&#146;s long-term debt. </FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px"><FONT SIZE="1">&nbsp;</FONT></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0px;margin-bottom:0px; text-indent:4%"><FONT
FACE="Times New Roman" SIZE="2">During the fourth quarter of fiscal 2004 we recorded an income tax benefit of $540,000 related to recognition of deferred tax assets primarily resulting from net operating loss carry forwards. </FONT></P> <P
